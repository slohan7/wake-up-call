Founder Daily Brief Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Founder Daily Brief is a personal/internal service that sends transactional SMS messages containing a daily founder brief and related operational notifications to a single opted-in mobile number controlled by the account owner.

Program Description

Founder Daily Brief sends operational, non-marketing SMS messages such as daily summaries, setup smoke tests, and occasional delivery verification messages. The service is not used for promotional campaigns or third-party advertising.

Privacy Policy

Founder Daily Brief may collect and use the following information to operate the service:

  • The recipient mobile phone number used to deliver the brief.
  • Message content needed to generate and send the daily brief.
  • Delivery metadata such as timestamps, provider message identifiers, and delivery status.
  • Operational context used to generate the brief, such as calendar, email, tasks, and follow-up records.

This information is used only to operate, debug, and improve the Founder Daily Brief service for the account owner. Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Founder Daily Brief may use service providers such as Twilio for message delivery and cloud or AI providers strictly to operate the service. These providers only receive the data required to perform their role.

Terms & Conditions

By opting in to Founder Daily Brief messaging, you agree to receive transactional SMS messages related to your daily brief and system operation.

  • Message frequency: typically 1 message per day, with occasional additional setup or test messages.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • To opt out, reply STOP.
  • For help, reply HELP.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
